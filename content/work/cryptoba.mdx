## Overview

Crypto.ba is a decentralized crypto community platform that serves as the main blockchain hub for enthusiasts, developers, and educators across Bosnia and Herzegovina. I joined the core team to help with development, forum maintenance, and community education — with a mission to promote blockchain awareness and self-custody tools throughout the region.

## My Role

- Maintained the **forum infrastructure** (Discourse-based), including security patches, backups, and plugin customization.
- Developed **community tools** (wallet explorers, bot integrations, crypto utilities).
- Assisted with **education and outreach**, including AMAs, writing guides, and participating in local events.
- Collaborated with local builders on emerging projects and hackathons.

## Highlights

- Helped grow the Crypto.ba platform to **thousands of active members**, making it the largest regional crypto hub.
- Participated in organizing meetups and partnering on events like **MOSTART**, **Hackathons**, and academic panels.
- Provided **multi-language technical support**, helping onboard hundreds of users into safe crypto practices.

## Tech & Tools

- **Discourse**, **Docker**, **Ubuntu**, **Nginx**
- Custom bots in **Node.js** & **Python**
- Self-hosted tools for privacy and autonomy

## Why it Matters

Crypto.ba isn’t a startup — it’s a resilient community that empowers individuals with open-source software, education, and peer support. By working on Crypto.ba, I’ve learned the value of **grassroots organizing**, **digital sovereignty**, and maintaining high-trust, high-autonomy systems over the long term.
