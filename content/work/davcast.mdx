## Overview

DavCast is a podcast platform highlighting technology, crypto, and innovation stories in Bosnia and Herzegovina. I contributed to the platform’s development and content ecosystem, helping it reach audiences interested in tech, blockchain, and entrepreneurship.

## My Role

- Assisted with **website development** and platform maintenance, ensuring smooth playback and accessibility.
- Developed **tools and integrations** for publishing and community engagement.
- Supported **content creation and outreach**, including coordinating guest appearances and participating in discussions.
- Collaborated with creators and local innovators to highlight emerging projects and trends.

## Highlights

- Helped the platform reach a **growing audience of tech and crypto enthusiasts** in the region.
- Participated in producing and organizing episodes with influential speakers.
- Implemented **user-friendly features** to simplify access to content and engagement.

## Tech & Tools

- **Next.js**, **React**, **Vercel**
- Custom scripts in **Node.js** & **Python**
- Web audio and streaming optimizations

## Why it Matters

DavCast bridges the gap between local innovators and global audiences. By working on the platform, I’ve learned the importance of **accessible content**, **community building**, and **technical reliability** in media-focused projects.
