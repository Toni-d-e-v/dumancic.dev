## Overview

Let's Go Fitness Mostar is a modern fitness center located in the heart of Mostar, Bosnia and Herzegovina. I developed their complete digital presence from scratch, including a responsive website, membership management system, and online booking platform. The project transformed their operations from traditional paper-based processes to a fully digital experience for both staff and members.

## My Role

- Built the responsive website with modern React/Next.js stack, featuring multilingual support (Bosnian/Croatian/English).
- Developed membership management system with automated billing, package tracking, and member portal.
- Created platform for group classes, personal training sessions, and facility reservations.

## Highlights

- Launched a fully responsive website that increased online inquiries by 300% in the first quarter.
- Streamlined membership operations, reducing administrative work by 60% through automation.
- Integrated team showcase featuring 6 certified trainers with their qualifications and specializations.
- Implemented flexible pricing system supporting monthly, student, and combo packages.

## Why it Matters

Let's Go Fitness isn't just a gym — it's a community hub promoting health and wellness in Mostar. By digitizing their operations, I helped them focus on what matters most: providing excellent training and support to their members. The platform has enabled them to scale their services while maintaining the personal touch that makes them special in the local fitness scene.
