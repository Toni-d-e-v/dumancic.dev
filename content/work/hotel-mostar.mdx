## Overview

**Hotel Mostar** is a newly built four-star hotel in the very centre of Mostar — 103 accommodation units, three conference halls and a restaurant, ten minutes from the bus and railway stations and a short walk from the Old Bridge. The site presents the whole property with an elegant, editorial look: serif display headings, warm photography and a calm, hospitality-first tone.

## Key Features

### Presentation
- **Full room catalogue** — double and triple rooms, Superior and Presidential apartments, each with size, capacity and amenity lists
- **Conference & events** — three halls (boardroom to 300-seat congress) plus a banquet hall and weddings section
- **Dining** — restaurant and caffé-pâtisserie with terrace, including opening hours
- **Amenities grid, gallery and location** section with distances to key points

### Product
- **Bilingual EN / HR** content with a language switcher
- **Reservation flows** — direct reserve CTAs and per-room links to Booking.com
- **SEO-ready** metadata and semantic structure
- Fully responsive, fast static delivery on Vercel

## Tech Stack

- **Next.js + TypeScript**
- **Tailwind CSS**
- **Vercel** deployment
