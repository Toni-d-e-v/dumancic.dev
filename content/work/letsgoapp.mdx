## Overview

LetsGo Fitness is a smart fitness and nutrition app designed to guide users step-by-step toward their goals, whether at home or in the gym. Using intelligent algorithms, it generates **personalized workout and nutrition plans** tailored to each user’s needs, goals, and lifestyle, helping them transform their body, mind, and habits effectively.

## My Role

- Developed **adaptive workout and nutrition algorithms** that generate personalized 4-week cycles based on user data.
- Implemented **progress tracking systems** for weight, body fat percentage, strength, and workout completion.
- Built **user interfaces** for seamless experience on mobile and web platforms.
- Collaborated on **content integration**, including exercise video demonstrations and meal planning features.

## Highlights

- Created a platform supporting **customizable plans for beginners, intermediates, and advanced users**.
- Integrated over **200 exercises with detailed instructions and video demos**.
- Developed **automatic meal planning and grocery list features**, tailored to individual goals.
- Enabled **real-time progress tracking** with graphs and analytics to motivate users.

## Tech & Tools

- **React Native**, **Next.js**, **Node.js**
- **Firebase** for backend and real-time data storage
- **Stripe** integration for subscription management
- Video hosting and streaming for exercise demos

## Why it Matters

LetsGo Fitness empowers users to take control of their health with **personalized guidance, automated planning, and easy tracking**. Working on this project taught me the importance of combining **smart algorithms, UX design, and user motivation systems** to create a product that truly adapts to each individual’s fitness journey.
