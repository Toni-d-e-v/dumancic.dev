## Overview

**Solie Naturals Luxury** is a premium natural cosmetics brand focused on gentle, effective skincare for sensitive and reactive skin.  
I built and launched the official Solie web shop with a strong emphasis on **trust, clarity, performance, and a luxury feel**, while keeping the buying flow simple and accessible.

The site serves as both a **brand presentation platform** and a **fully functional e-commerce store** for direct-to-customer sales.

## My Role

- Designed and developed the complete **WordPress + WooCommerce** web shop.
- Implemented **Elementor-based layout system** for easy content editing and scalability.
- Configured **secure online payments** (Visa & Mastercard).
- Optimized the site for **performance, SEO, and mobile devices**.
- Structured product pages to clearly communicate **ingredients, benefits, and usage**.
- Set up blog, FAQ, legal pages, and multilingual-ready structure.

## Highlights

- Clean, luxury-oriented UI aligned with Solie’s brand identity.
- Clear product storytelling focused on **natural ingredients and skin safety**.
- Fast and intuitive checkout experience.
- Built-in blog for education and long-term organic growth.
- Fully production-ready store with real customers and orders.

## Tech & Tools

- **WordPress**
- **WooCommerce**
- **Elementor**
- **PHP**, **HTML**, **CSS**
- Payment gateways (Visa / Mastercard)
- Basic SEO & performance optimization
