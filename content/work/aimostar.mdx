## Overview

AI Mostar conference was a youth-driven initiative born out of the Spark project (Skilled Youth Empowered Communities) by the British Council. The event aimed to gather young professionals, students, and enthusiasts passionate about artificial intelligence to share knowledge, exchange ideas, and inspire the next generation.

Alongside other experts like Edin Mehić and Kerim Balić, I played a key role as both an organizer and speaker, helping coordinate the event held at Hotel Mostar, and delivering a presentation on AI applications and blockchain technology.

## Event Highlights

- **Four dynamic lectures** by experts including myself, Edin Mehić, Nedžad Smajić, Kerim Balić, and Marko Buhovac, covering AI trends, practical use cases, and future technologies.
- Interactive **networking session** for attendees to connect and discuss ideas.
- Free entry supported by generous sponsors like British Council, Crypto.ba, Bloomteq, and ZK Office.
- Over 100 attendees participated in this first edition, sparking conversations about the future of AI in Bosnia and Herzegovina.

## Impact

This conference was the result of two months of intensive preparation by a team of motivated high school students and young professionals. It created a valuable platform to foster AI awareness and build a local community committed to technology and innovation.

## Why It Matters

Empowering youth with knowledge and resources in AI is essential for building a forward-looking society. AI Mostar represents the spirit of grassroots innovation and collaboration, paving the way for Bosnia's role in the digital transformation era.
